# Photobooth Quick Guide - What's New? 🎉

## 🚀 Major Improvements

Your photobooth just got a **massive upgrade**! Here's what's new:

---

## 1. 🪞 Selfie Mirror (Camera Flip)

**What it does:** Front camera now shows you mirrored, like looking in a mirror

**Why it matters:** Makes taking selfies natural and easy - you see yourself the way you expect!

**How to use:** Just use front camera - it's automatic

---

## 2. 🔄 Switch Cameras

**What it does:** Toggle between front and back camera anytime

**Why it matters:** Choose the best angle without starting over

**How to use:** Look for the 🔄 button while camera is active

---

## 3. 👁️ Photo Review & Retake

**What it does:** See each photo after capture and decide to keep or retake

**Why it matters:** No more bad photos! Every photo can be perfect

**How to use:**
- After each photo, you see a preview
- Click **"✓ Залишити"** to keep it
- Click **"🔁 Переробити"** to take it again
- Or wait 3 seconds to auto-continue

---

## 4. 🎵 Sound Effects

**What it does:** Beeps for countdown, shutter click for capture, success melody when done

**Why it matters:** More engaging and fun experience

**How to use:**
- Toggle sounds on/off from start screen: **Звуки 🔊/🔇**
- Sounds are ON by default

---

## 5. 📊 Progress Bar

**What it does:** Visual indicator showing how many photos taken

**Why it matters:** Always know where you are in the session

**How to use:** Just look at the blue bar at top - it fills as you progress

---

## 6. ✨ Better Animations

**What it does:** Smooth transitions, pulsing countdown, fading thumbnails

**Why it matters:** Professional, polished feel

**How to use:** Just enjoy the smoother experience!

---

## 📱 How to Use (Step by Step)

### Step 1: Start
Click **"Почати"** button

### Step 2: Choose Format
- **Портрет** (📱) - Vertical photos
- **Ландшафт** (🖼️) - Horizontal photos
- **Квадрат** (⬜) - Square photos

### Step 3: Choose Source
- **Зробити фото** (📷) - Use camera
- **Завантажити** (📂) - Upload from gallery

### Step 4: Camera
- Wait for camera to initialize
- Click **🔄** to switch front/back camera if needed
- Click **"Почати зйомку"** when ready

### Step 5: Photo Session
- Watch countdown (3, 2, 1...)
- Smile! Photo captures automatically
- **NEW:** Review photo - keep or retake
- Repeat for all photos

### Step 6: Preview
- See your final layout
- Download as PNG or JPG
- Or take new photos

## 💡 Pro Tips

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Camera won't start?
- Check browser permissions
- Allow camera access when prompted
- Try refreshing the page

### Can't switch cameras?
- Device must have multiple cameras
- Some browsers limit camera access

### Sounds not working?
- Check sound toggle is ON (🔊)
- Check device volume
- Some browsers block autoplay audio

### Photo review not showing?
- It only shows between photos (not after last one)
- Auto-continues after 3 seconds
- You can click "Keep" to skip waiting
